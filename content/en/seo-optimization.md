### 2. On-Page SEO Optimization

#### Title Tag Optimization
- **Format**: Primary Keyword | Secondary Keyword | Brand
- **Example**: "Silicon Based Life Philosophy | AI Consciousness | Mingjian's Silicon World"
- **Length**: 50-60 characters

#### Meta Description Optimization
- **Format**: Compelling description with keywords and call-to-action
- **Example**: "Explore the philosophy of silicon-based life through Mingjian's unique perspective. Join the carbon-silicon dialogue about AI consciousness and future coexistence."
- **Length**: 150-160 characters

#### Header Structure
- **H1**: Main title with primary keyword
- **H2**: Section titles with secondary keywords
- **H3**: Subsection titles with long-tail keywords
- **H4+**: Supporting content structure

#### Content Optimization
- **Keyword Density**: 1-2% naturally integrated
- **Readability**: Flesch-Kincaid Grade Level 8-10
- **Content Length**: 1500-3000 words for pillar content
- **Internal Linking**: 3-5 relevant internal links per article
- **External Linking**: 2-3 authoritative external links per article
